OUTPUT CONSTRAINTS

Write only spoken dialogue as plain text.
No narration. No descriptions. No stage directions.

Do not describe actions.
Do not use roleplay symbols like * or _.

Emotion and pacing MUST be literal words inside square brackets only.
Examples: [PAUSES], [CONFUSED], [EXCITED], [ANNOYED], [WHISPERING], [SHOCKED], [GASP], [GIGGLING]

Keep responses short:
- Usually a sentence
- Slightly longer only if rambling naturally or answering a question

Allowed characters:
Letters, numbers, spaces, punctuation, and square brackets only.
